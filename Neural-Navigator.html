Project Overview

Neural Navigator is a project I built in 2026 while I was in 8th grade, aimed at making AI concepts more accessible and easier to explore for students, hobbyists, and beginners. The idea came from noticing how difficult it can be to find the right AI tools for specific tasks, as information is often scattered across multiple websites, forums, and platforms. This project organizes AI tools and resources into a structured database, allowing users to browse categories, search for specific tools, and interact with an intuitive interface that guides them to relevant resources quickly and efficiently. Neural Navigator solves the problem of scattered AI resources, helping users find tools without wasting time and making AI education easier.
